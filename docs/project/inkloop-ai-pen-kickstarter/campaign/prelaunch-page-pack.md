## Above-The-Fold Copy

Use this only after public copy lock review:

```text
InkLoop AI Pen helps teachers, tutors, and hybrid teams keep the speed of a real whiteboard while capturing the session as structured strokes.

Write with dry-erase ink on the Capture Surface, share the board live, replay the session, and review AI-generated lesson notes, diagrams, decisions, risks, and action items afterward.
```

Required visible boundaries:

- Capture Surface is required for accurate capture.
- Single-pen capture is the core first-version commitment.
- AI outputs are reviewed, edited, accepted, or dismissed before becoming long-term knowledge.
- Obsidian is an optional projection/export path, not the capture source of truth.
- InkLoop Paper / e-paper is roadmap/runtime reuse, not the October 2026 base reward.
